Project Idea

My IT project idea is an application which can be downloaded onto the user’s smartphone via the app store and used as a form of social media. My idea which I have come up with revolves around connecting people who share a love for animals. It will be tailored for those who are looking for someone who can sit their pets if they are in an emergency, or just need someone they can trust who shares the same passion as them or perhaps just planning a walk in the local area with other dog owners, as a way to build friendships with neighbours.

My motivation for this idea comes from two main sources. I personally struggle to create friendships and interact socially, but from experience I’ve had positive interactions meeting others who also have pets and although it may seem crazy or odd to someone who might not feel the same, I know its defiantly a thing that other people can relate to. My other motivation for this application is to encourage more people to walk their dogs and perhaps make it more appealing if the “task” of walking a pet can be tied together with social interactions.

Each user on the application will have their own personal profile with personal information about themselves and their pets. The user will be able to fully customize their personal profiles with text and images and see the profiles of those who live nearby them via gps technology. This will give the user I sense of comfortability knowing there are like minded people out there who share the same passion.

An app development software will be needed, such as Quixy, AppyPie, iBuildApp etc. It will be a requirement having a great understanding of software development, specifically in web app development. The skills required for such a project include product management: the lead for a mobile app developper needs to dig into the business side of the app and its needs; user interface and user experience: this is necessary to be able to present your idea to potential users; communication: there may be many app developers working on the project and it important that every member is on the same page.
